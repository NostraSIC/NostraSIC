Identity

Who are we?

Nostra Social Investments (NSI Consultants) is a global consulting organization, providing corporate social investment (CSI) solutions in order to strengthen communities and improve corporate citizenship.

As an organization, we are driven by the need to accommodate business interests with the essentials of human development. NSI Consultants develops projects and administers its services for clients in a manner that caters to their capacity. We also operate in a cost-efficient approach that is beneficial for everyone.

Goal

Our Vision

The end goal in our work is determined by our most important client: our communities.

We put all our effort to:

  • - Provide consultancy services that engage corporate businesses alongside NGOs on how to fully utilize resources for growth in both business and the community.
  • - Designing a network that sees governments, NGO’s, businesses and communities constantly engaging and formulating shared interests and values.
  • - Expand our presence in order to increase our pool of providers to support initiatives across the globe.
  • - Transform the culture in which corporate citizenship is carried out through a triple bottom line (TBL)-- environmental, financial, and social return on investment.
Creed

Our Values

Our culture and values help us fulfill our purpose

  • Sustainability ~ Changing the culture in which businesses, NGO’s, and individuals alike achieve a viable atmosphere.
  • Transparency ~ Creating a trustworthy and rewarding relationship between donors, investors, and recipients on any given project.
  • Innovation ~ Developing new CSI/CSR models through a borderless exchange of finances, knowledge, and additional resources.
Quality

Our Services

NSI Consultants provides tailor made packages for all social investment (and development) stakeholders to ensure that all parties meet the objectives and create social change.

  • - Project Identification and Benchmarking for CSI initiatives.
  • - Developing Annual CSR/Sustainability Reports based on monitoring targets and data.
  • - Civic Education and Training for employees.
  • - Fundraising & Planning Assistance: Helping NGO’s to maximize donor funds.

Our team will work with organizations to enhance their projects, and identify businesses willing to become active donors and participate. We will also cooperate with businesses on how to be more cost-efficient with their annual CSR budgets.

Reason

Why Us?

What makes NSI Consultants distinct is that our staff utilizes the transfer and exchange of resources, finances and knowledge- creating a borderless CSI/CSR model.

We are aware that CSI spending has the potential to be cost-efficient and effective for stakeholders. Our model combines intuition with innovation for a hands-on approach by our team.
