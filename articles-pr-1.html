NSI Consultants partners with iLabs

  • Oct 2,2023
  • Admin
  • News

    Opportunity opens for NSI Europe to
    work hands-on with social impact projects.

Oslo, Norway; Oct 2, 2023:

NSI Consultants and iLabs announced that the companies have

entered into a strategic partnership that will help bring social impact projects into new markets,

such as Europe, while also providing technology and resources to help scale these projects.

The agreement brings together two businesses that will help social enterprises operate across

different countries in order to bring innovative products to new audiences. The partnership will

also focus on bringing Norwegian values to the Americas and South Africa, while building

cross-cultural relationships.


“We are looking forward to working with NSI Consultants and collaborating on innovative projects,"

said Achmad Nasir, Founder and Chairman of the Board at iLabs.


Clients of NSI Consultants in the Americas will now be able to access funding from European

and Scandinavian organizations, while having a larger platform to spread their social

enterprises to audiences in Europe and South Africa.


“We believe that this is an opportunity for our partners and clients to explore how they can

utilize and further develop their tech platforms,”said Alan Smith, Director of International

Operations at NSI Consultants.“Collaborating with iLabs provides us a way forward to work in Scandinavia with our sister group, NSI Europe.”
